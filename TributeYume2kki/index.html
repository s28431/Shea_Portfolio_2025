WHAT IS YUME 2KKI?

Yume 2kki is a collaborative fan game based on Yume Nikki where you explore your dreams. Yume 2kki focuses on exploration gameplay, as you explore you can find effects, get badges, and trigger events/endings. Since it is made collaboratively you get to see so many cool creative ideas from a variety of people as you explore.

WHO IS UROTSUKI?

Urotsuki is the main character of Yume 2kki, her name means to wander aimlessly. When you being the game you start in your room, when trying to leave through the bedroom door Urotsuki will shake her head, refusing to exit. The only real option from there is to either play the mini games on Urotsuki's console or to fall asleep and begin explore the vast dream world.
